scratch

interactive english project

in my english class, i do biweekly assignments where i read two chapters from a book and write a paper that includes a summary of those two chapters and a short essay following a prompt that correlates with one of the assignments. my partner, joaquin ancona, and i turned this assignment interactive using python, breaking it down and allowing the user to choose what they want to read from my essay. joaquin and i also made a flowchart to help us with planning, which you can find here (the flowchart a bit hard to see, so for best results, open the file in draw.io, which is accessible when you hover over the top of the screen).
